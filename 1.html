Analogram이란?

All my life, I've tried to shoot the decisive moment of life, every moment of life was the decisive moment.

Analogram which combines the words 'Analog' and 'RAM' Translates in a literal sense to an analog saving book. We are all living within a concept we call time As we live our lives we collect memories along that line

We can easily numb ourselves from our human emotions and relationships. So. in light of this, Aboview had turned to the past to tap into the future. This project aims to capture those moments So the colors of those time are vivid And the intimate details are within it What we have created is a tribute to everyone's past present and future. A tangible sentiment that will protect your memories for a lifetime. This one is for the books. This is Analogram
